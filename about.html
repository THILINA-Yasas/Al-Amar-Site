About Us

Al Amal Manpower Agency is a trusted recruitment and staffing agency based in Kuwait, specializing in connecting skilled and unskilled workers with top employers across various industries. With a commitment to excellence, we provide reliable, efficient, and ethical manpower solutions to meet the growing demands of businesses in Kuwait and beyond.

At Al Amal Manpower Agency, we take pride in shaping careers and supporting businesses by delivering the right talent at the right time. Whether you are an employer looking for the best workforce or a job seeker searching for the right opportunity, we are here to help! Contact Us Today and let’s build a better workforce together!

Our Process

This is Our Process

Job Requirement Analysis & Candidate Sourcing

We collaborate with employers to define job roles, qualifications, and requirements.

Interview & Skills Assessment

Shortlisted candidates undergo a structured interview process, which may include in person face-to-face meeting, skill tests, or practical evaluations to ensure they meet job expectations.

Medical & Visa Processing

Once selected, candidates must complete a mandatory medical examination for work clearance.

Travel, Onboarding & Post-Employment Support

After visa approval, we arrange flight tickets and pre-departure briefings. Upon arrival in Kuwait, candidates receive work orientation, residency permit assistance, and continued support to ensure a smooth transition.
